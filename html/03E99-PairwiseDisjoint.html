pairwise disjoint


Definition Suppose {Eα∣α∈I} is an arbitrary collection of sets. These sets are said to be pairwise disjoint if for every pair of distinct elements α,β∈I, we have Eα∩Eβ=∅.

Remark

The synonym mutually disjoint is also used.
