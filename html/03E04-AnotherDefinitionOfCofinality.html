another definition of cofinality


Let κ be a limit ordinal (e.g. a cardinal). The cofinality of κ cf⁡(κ) could also be defined as:

cf⁡(κ)=inf⁡{|U|:U⊆κ⁢ such that ⁢sup⁡U=κ}

(sup⁡U is calculated using the natural order of the ordinals). The cofinality of a cardinal is always a regular cardinal and hence cf⁡(κ)=cf⁡(cf⁡(κ)).

This definition is equivalent to the parent definition.
