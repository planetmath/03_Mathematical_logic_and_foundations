partitions less than cofinality


If λ<cf⁡(κ) then κ→(κ)λ1.

This follows easily from the definition of cofinality. For any coloring f:κ→λ then define g:λ→κ+1 by g⁢(α)=|f-1⁢(α)|. Then κ=∑α<λg⁢(α), and by the normal rules of cardinal arithmetic supα<λ⁡g⁢(α)=κ. Since λ<cf⁡(κ), there must be some α<λ such that g⁢(α)=κ.
